Coordinate Plane Battleship


How To Play

  1. This is a 2 player game where the objective is to sink the opponents battleships.
  2. Each player must separately choose 2 types of battleships:
    • One ship must be parallel to the x-axis or parallel to the y-axis.
    • The remainig ship must be angled 45° from the x-axis with a slope of positive 1 or negative 1.
    • Battleships may not overlap onto each other.
    • Battleships may not lie on the x or y axes.
  3. Player 1 must place their battleships in Quadrant I and/or Quadrant III while player 2 looks away.
  4. Player 2 must place their battleships in Quadrant II and/or Quadrant IV while player 1 looks away.
  5. Once ships have been chosen and placed, the battle may begin.
  6. Players must take turns finding the ordered pair where the enemy battleships are located to fire a torpedo.
  7. In order to sink a battleship, players must hit all partitions (points) of the battleship using their torpedoes.
  8. The first player to sink all partitions (points) of their opponents battleships wins.

Battleship Types:

Aircraft Carrier - 4 points

Cruiser - 4 points



Destroyer - 3 points



Submarine - 3 points
